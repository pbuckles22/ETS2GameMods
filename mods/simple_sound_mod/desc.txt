Simple Sound Mod
================

A minimal example showing the bare minimum needed for a sound mod.

This mod demonstrates that you DON'T need all the folders - 
just the ones relevant to your content type.

Features:
- Minimal file structure
- Only required files included
- Sound-specific category

Installation:
1. Build with: ./scripts/build.sh simple_sound_mod
2. Copy dist/simple_sound_mod.scs to Documents/Euro Truck Simulator 2/mod/
3. Enable in Mod Manager

To add your music:
1. Create a sound/music/ folder
2. Add your .ogg files
